Taxi fare rate hike proposal

Facts

  • Fares in Chicago have not risen in 8 years.
  • 24th least expensive cab fare of 40 major cities.
  • Cost of living has increased 17.8% over the past eight years.
  • Most drivers spend 11 to 13 hours per day driving.
  • Average income after expenses is $4-7 an hour.
  • Total daily income after expenses is $44-91.
  • Chicago is short 2000 cab drivers.
